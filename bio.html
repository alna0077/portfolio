Profile

Emad has contributed 11+ years of professional experience in the IT and cybersecurity field. The commitment and willingness to deliver high-quality services allowed him to be promoted from helpdesk agent to system administrator, and then was recommended to be a project manager after the first two years of his professional experience, he worked these three roles with the largest IT service provider in the country. Later he joined a cybersecurity defense firm as a Senior Project Manager and after that promoted to be a Team Lead of the Project Management Office. As a project manager, Emad has worked with public and private sectors and delivered projects to multiple types of industries including but not limited to health, banking, insurance, education, and military. He has extensive experience with project methodologies, like Microsoft Solution Framework (MSF) and the PMI five-phase methodology, in addition to the Information Technology Infrastructure Library (ITIL) Framework, NIST, and ISO security standard and framework. Emad has managed medium to enterprise-level projects with a total budget of more than USD 20 million. He worked closely and managed different sizes of teams up to a few dozens of professionals that included employment engineers, network administrators, system architects, developers, and cybersecurity consultants. Emad demonstrates extensive experience in managing all phases of projects starting with the initiation and going through design, execution, and control, and ending with the closing phase. He develops and prepares project management plans that include schedule, budget, stakeholder, resource, communication, acceptance, escalation, and quality plans. He identifies stakeholders and conducts kick-off and periodic meetings. Working closely with the teams, he facilitates scope delivery, administers scope changes, makes sure deadlines are met and the cost is maintained. His risk assessment competency helps in identifying risks and preparing mitigation plans.
